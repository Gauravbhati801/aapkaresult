SSC CGL 2026 New Vacancy Online Form

Post Date: 10 June 2026 | Official Notice Date: 21 May 2026

Short Details: Staff Selection Commission (SSC) ne Combined Graduate Level Examination, 2026 ke liye notification जारी किया है। Is recruitment ke through Central Government Ministries/Departments/Organizations me Group B aur Group C posts par approx. 12,256 tentative vacancies भरी जाएंगी. Eligible graduate candidates 22 June 2026 tak online form submit kar sakte hain.

  • Online Apply Start Date : 21 May 2026
  • Online Apply Last Date : 22 June 2026 up to 11:00 PM

  • Correction Window : 29 June 2026 to 01 July 2026

Application Fee
  • General / OBC / EWS : ₹100/-
  • SC / ST / PwBD / Ex-Servicemen : ₹0/-

  • Correction Charge First Time : ₹200/-
  • Correction Charge Second Time : ₹500/-
  • Payment Mode : BHIM UPI / Net Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card
SSC CGL 2026 : Age Limit as on 01 August 2026
Post-wise age limit 18-27 years, 20-30 years, 18-30 years aur 18-32 years tak hai. SC/ST ko 5 years, OBC ko 3 years aur PwBD candidates ko rules ke अनुसार relaxation मिलेगा.
SSC CGL 2026 : Educational Qualification
Basic eligibility ke liye candidate ke paas recognized University/Institute se Bachelor's Degree honi chahiye. Junior Statistical Officer aur Statistical Investigator posts ke liye additional Mathematics/Statistics related conditions apply hoti hain; apply karne se pehle official notification जरूर पढ़ें.
