Indian Air Force (IAF) has released the notification for Agniveer Vayu Intake 01/2027 under the Agnipath Scheme. Application Fee

  • Payment Mode (Online) :
  • Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / UPI
Indian Airforce Agniveer Notification 2026 : Age Limit
Minimum Age : 17.5 Years | Maximum Age : 21 Years
Candidates born between specified dates as per IAF rules are eligible to apply.
IAF Agniveer Vayu 01/2027 : Physical Eligibility Details
Height : Minimum Acceptable Height is 152.5 CMS for Male & 152 CMS for Female Candidates.
Chest : Minimum range of expansion: 5 cm
Running : 1.6 Km in 07 Minutes (Male) and 08 Minutes (Female).
Indian Airforce Agniveer Vayu : Education Qualification
Subject Name Indian Airforce Agniveer Eligibility
Science Subjects ✔ 10+2 Intermediate with Mathematics, Physics, and English with Minimum 50% Marks in Aggregate and 50% Marks in English.

✔ OR 3 Year Diploma in Engineering (Mechanical / Electrical / Electronics / Automobiles / Computer Science / Instrumentation Technology / Information Technology) with Minimum 50% Marks and 50% Marks in English.

✔ OR 2 Year Vocation Course with Non-Vocational Subject Physics and Math.
Other Than Science Subjects ✔ 10+2 Intermediate in Any Subject with Minimum 50% Marks in Aggregate and 50% Marks in English.

✔ OR 2 Year Vocation Course with Minimum 50% Aggregate and 50% Marks in English.
