Privacy Policy (गोपनीयता नीति)

Effective Date: 24 May 2026
Last Updated: 24 May 2026

AapkaResult.in par aapka trust humare liye sabse important hai. Is policy me hum clearly batate hain ki website use karte waqt kaunsa data collect hota hai, kyun hota hai, aur aap us par kaise control rakh sakte hain.

1) Hum kya collect karte hain

  • Basic technical logs: IP address, browser type, device type, visited pages, date/time.
  • Contact form data (agar aap message bhejte hain): naam, email, message content.
  • Performance analytics: page speed, user flow, bounce behavior (aggregated level par).

2) Data collect karne ka purpose

  • Website performance improve karna (speed, design, navigation).
  • Content quality improve karna (which pages are useful, which need updates).
  • Spam, bot abuse aur security threats ko monitor karna.

3) Cookies aur ad personalization

Future me agar ads serve kiye jate hain, to third-party vendors (jaise Google) cookies ka use kar sakte hain. Aap browser settings se cookies disable kar sakte hain. Personalized ads se opt out ke liye aap Google Ad Settings use kar sakte hain.

4) Third-party links

Hum kai pages par official exam/recruitment websites ke links dete hain. Un websites ki privacy practices alag ho sakti hain. Isliye kisi bhi external website par data submit karne se pehle unki privacy policy जरूर padhein.

5) User rights

Aap humse request kar sakte hain ki contact form ke through bheja gaya aapka data review, update ya delete kiya jaye. Request bhejne ke liye contact@aapkaresult.in par email karein.

6) Children privacy

Website ka target competitive exam aspirants hain. Hum knowingly 13 saal se kam umra ke bacchon ka personal data collect nahi karte.

7) Policy updates

Policy time to time update ho sakti hai. Major updates ka date is page ke top par update kar diya jayega.
