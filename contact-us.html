Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) Before Contacting Us

To save your valuable time, please read these common queries before sending us an email:

  • Q1: Is AapkaResult.in an official government website?
    Ans: No. AapkaResult.in is an independent, privately run educational portal. We are NOT affiliated with the Government of India or any State Government. We only aggregate public information for educational purposes.

  • Q2: Can you help me fill out my online application form?
    Ans: Unfortunately, no. We provide the direct links and guides, but candidates must fill out their application forms themselves on the official government portals.

  • Q3: I paid my application fee, but my form is still showing 'Pending'. What should I do?
    Ans: We do not collect any fees. All payments are processed by the respective government recruitment boards (e.g., SSC, UPPRPB, RRB). You must contact the official helpline numbers provided in the respective exam notification.

  • Q4: Do you charge any money for your mock tests or job alerts?
    Ans: Absolutely not! All our job alerts, syllabus PDFs, and mock tests are 100% free for students. If anyone asks for money on behalf of AapkaResult, consider it a scam and report it to us immediately.
